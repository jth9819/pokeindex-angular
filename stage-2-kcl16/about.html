Launch Pad

Launch Pad is an interactive space launch tracking tool for space exploration enthusiasts. This app will allow users to find upcoming rocket launches from different oganizations around the world, track them as they approach launch day, and even watch them launch in real time. Users will also be able to find past launches and all data associated with the launch including recorded video footage of the launch.
